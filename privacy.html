Privacy Policy

AI Menu Translator · Last updated: March 2026

We built AI Menu Translator to help people understand menus anywhere in the world. Your privacy is important to us — we collect only what's necessary to make the app work.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our translation service:

  • Device ID — A randomly generated identifier used only to track your free translation usage. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.
  • Menu Text — Text captured from your camera or uploaded photos is sent to our server for translation and immediately discarded after processing. We do not store menu content.
  • Subscription Status — If you subscribe, Apple processes your payment. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status — no payment details.

2. Camera & Photos

AI Menu Translator requests access to your camera and photo library solely to capture menu text for translation. We do not store, upload, or share your photos or camera feed. Images are processed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework for text extraction.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To translate menu text via our AI-powered backend
  • To track free translation usage (5 free translations per device)
  • To verify active subscriptions and unlock unlimited translations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the translation service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Anthropic — Menu text is sent to Anthropic's API for AI translation. Anthropic processes this data according to their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — Subscription purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. We receive no payment information.
  • Railway — Our backend server is hosted on Railway. No personal data is stored on their servers beyond standard server logs.

5. Data Retention

  • Menu text — Never stored. Discarded immediately after translation.
  • Device ID & usage count — Stored in server memory only. Resets when the server restarts.
  • Translation history — Stored locally on your device only. We have no access to it.

6. Children's Privacy

AI Menu Translator is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal information to use.

7. Security

All communication between the app and our server is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We do not store sensitive data such as payment information, passwords, or personal identifiers.

8. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data and nothing is linked to your identity, there is effectively no personal profile to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns, please contact us and we will address them promptly.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Railway Privacy Policy

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
